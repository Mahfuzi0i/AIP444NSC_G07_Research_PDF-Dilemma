=== PAGE 2 ===
Signposts & Wayfinding

**Signposts**
* Features helping users figure out surroundings
* Page/window titles & web-page logos
* Branding devices, tabs & selection indicators
* Global & local navigation links
* Progress indicators & breadcrumbs
* Annotated scroll bars

**Wayfinding**
* What users do to find their way to goals
* Clear, unambiguous labels at each decision point
* Strong calls to action on first pages
* Environmental clues (X close, logos in corners)
* Maps provide mental picture of whole space
* Some users prefer sign-to-sign; others need overview
